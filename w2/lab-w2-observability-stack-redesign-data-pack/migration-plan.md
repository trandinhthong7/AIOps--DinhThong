# Eight-Week Migration Plan

Guarantee: Datadog, Splunk, and PagerDuty remain live until the replacement path has passed its gate. No business-hours cut-over removes the old path without a tested rollback. All cut-overs happen behind dual-write or mirror mode first.

| Week | Work | Cut-over | Rollback path | Go/no-go gate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | File Splunk non-renew notice, export all dashboards/alerts, freeze new vendor-specific alerts unless approved. Deploy OTel Collector in shadow mode on staging. | No production cut-over. | Remove staging collectors; current Datadog/Splunk/PagerDuty unchanged. | Inventory complete: 100% current monitors listed, 100% PagerDuty routes exported, Splunk notice filed. |
| 2 | Deploy OTel Collector DaemonSet to production with metrics/logs/traces mirrored to a sandbox. Enable PII filter and cardinality denylist for `customer_id`. | Mirror only; no alert or dashboard user changes. | Disable collector DaemonSet via Helm rollback; Datadog agents remain primary. | Collector drops <0.1% non-filtered telemetry, no app latency regression, cardinality report generated daily. |
| 3 | Stand up VictoriaMetrics + vmalert. Reproduce top 30 Datadog host/service alerts and SLO burn-rate dashboards in Grafana. | Shadow alerts compare with Datadog, no PagerDuty route yet. | Stop vmalert and keep Datadog monitors as paging source. | 95% of historical metric alerts reproduce within 2 minutes of Datadog timing; Grafana p95 dashboard load <3s. |
| 4 | Stand up Tempo and configure tail sampling: 100% errors/slow spans, 10% baseline. Instrument critical services: edge-lb, checkout-svc, payment-svc, catalog-db callers. | Grafana trace links added to runbooks, Datadog tracing remains primary. | Route traces back to Datadog only; leave Tempo read-only. | Synthetic payment cascade produces trace path edge-lb -> checkout -> payment -> payments-db with p99 outlier visible. |
| 5 | Stand up Loki hot tier and S3 archive. Route structured INFO/WARN/ERROR logs to Loki; retain raw compressed logs in S3. Rebuild five common Splunk searches. | On-call uses Loki for current-week incidents; Splunk remains available for all searches. | Flip collector exporter back to Datadog/Splunk-only and keep Loki read-only. | Loki query p99 <10s for 14-day service/severity/incident_id queries; saved search parity for top five runbooks. |
| 6 | Deploy correlation service and evidence ledger. Feed grouped alerts into a non-paging PagerDuty service. Run game day on payment pool exhaustion and catalog slow query. | Switch one low-risk service team to grouped PagerDuty events during office hours. | Route PagerDuty integration back to Datadog monitors directly within 30 minutes. | Game day: on-call forms first hypothesis under 5 minutes; no missed critical alerts; grouped event count reduced by >70%. |
| 7 | Expand grouped PagerDuty routing to all production services. Reduce PagerDuty active users from 65 to 35. Disable duplicate Datadog synthetics and deploy blackbox/k6 critical checks. | PagerDuty receives only correlation-service incidents for production service alerts. | Re-enable old PagerDuty integrations and restore previous user list from export. | 7-day canary: no missed pages, no >1 severity misroute, on-call survey says Grafana-first workflow is usable. |
| 8 | Disable Datadog APM/logs/custom-metric ingest and Grafana Cloud mirror. Keep Datadog infra metrics read-only for 14 days. Prepare Splunk export/contract exit package. | New stack is official source of record; old UIs are read-only fallback. | Re-enable Datadog agents/exporters via Helm and restore PagerDuty direct routes. | Finance projection shows >40% run-rate reduction; median first-hypothesis time in synthetic incidents <5.5m vs current 8m. |

## No-observability-blackout rule

Every signal has at least two live paths during its cut-over week. Metrics: Datadog + VictoriaMetrics in weeks 3-8. Logs: Splunk + Loki in weeks 5-8 and Splunk until contract exit. Traces: Datadog + Tempo in weeks 4-8. Paging: old PagerDuty integrations + grouped service during week 6 canary, then old integrations kept disabled but restorable for 14 days.
